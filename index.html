About Us

Weddings have proven to be a large and widespread creative industry in Indonesia. Born with the idea that wedding preparations is one of the most important moments in everyone's life and with its mission to connect each and all people from individuals who are ready to get married to the wedding industry through media, shops and wedding exhibitions, Moonlight was founded to be a wedding media, the leading and most integrated agency and other services in Indonesia. Through online wedding portals, content and publishing divisions, wedding exhibitions and online wedding stores.
